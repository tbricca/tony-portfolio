About Me.

I am passionate about companies that are innovating industries and wanted to learn in depth about how technology is able to do that. I left my job at a startup to learn just that at General Assembly's web devlopment immersive bootcamp. After graduating, I now work as a freelance developer along with building out my own ideas.

Other interests of mine include: learning about public and private transportation, hiking, carpentry, running, watching sports, reading about history, and traveling
